Design Styles / Tools

Below are UI styles and approaches I often use depending on the project. Choose the style and I can adapt the UI to it.

Minimalism & Swiss Style

Clean, white space, functional, grid-based, monochrome with accent colors.

Neumorphism

Soft UI with subtle shadows and rounded corners — tactile and calm feeling.

Glassmorphism

Frosted glass backgrounds, translucent layers, and depth with blurred backdrops.

Brutalism

Bold, raw, high contrast, and intentionally unstyled for a disruptive aesthetic.

3D & Hyperrealism

Rich textures, deep colors and 3D models for immersive, tactile UIs.

Vibrant & Block-based

Colorful blocks, bold type and crisp geometric layouts for energetic UIs.

Dark Mode (OLED Optimized)

OLED-optimized dark themes with high readability and low glare.

Accessible & Ethical

WCAG-friendly UIs with keyboard navigation and accessible patterns.

Claymorphism / Aurora / Retro-future

Playful clay-like UI, aurora gradients, and retro-future neon aesthetics where appropriate.
